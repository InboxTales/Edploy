CA FOUNDATION COURSE

The CA Foundation Course is the entry-level examination for aspiring Chartered Accountants in India. It covers fundamental concepts in key areas such as:

  • Accounting
  • Law
  • Mathematics
  • Economics

The course is designed to provide a strong foundation for future CA studies and can be completed in 6 months. It is open to students who have completed their 12th grade.

Registration Fee: ₹9000

The CA Foundation course consists of 4 papers, totaling 400 marks. Each paper covers essential subjects including:

  • Accounting
  • Business Laws
  • Economics
  • Mathematics

To pass the course, students must secure at least 200 marks overall, with a minimum of 40% in each paper. This course is the first step in your journey to becoming a Chartered Accountant, providing a strong foundation in finance and commerce.

Intermediate Course

The Intermediate Course is the second level of the CA program, providing in-depth knowledge of accounting, taxation, and finance. The course prepares students for advanced professional challenges.
Duration: 9 months
Eligibility: Students who have passed the CA Foundation or hold a graduate/postgraduate degree.
Registration Fee: ₹18,000 for both groups, ₹13,000 for one group.

The Intermediate course includes two groups of 8 papers. To pass, students need at least 40% in each paper and a 50% overall aggregate. Completing this level allows progression to the CA Final course.

Final Course

The CA Final Course is the last stage of the CA journey, offering advanced expertise in financial reporting, strategic management, and taxation.
Duration: 6-12 months
Eligibility: Completion of both groups in Intermediate and practical training.
Registration Fee: ₹22,000.

The Final course consists of two groups of 8 papers. To qualify, students must score 40% in each paper and 50% overall. Completing this course is a major step toward becoming a Chartered Accountant.
